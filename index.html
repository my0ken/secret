Confession

Dear Migs,

Hello! If you’re reading this, baka may idea ka na about what this is, pero I’ll say it honestly and chill.

The first time we met, hindi ko in-expect na may isang taong sobrang bubbly and friendly agad sa akin. You made me feel welcome, even if bago ako sa environment niyo, and I genuinely appreciated that.

Over time, nagkaroon ako ng feelings for you. Hindi ko naman siya planado or hinanap...nangyari lang habang mas nakilala kita.

But I really value our friendship, and I don’t want this to feel heavy or awkward for you. You don’t have to say anything right now or at all. Whatever you feel, okay lang talaga, and I’ll adapt.

Gusto ko lang maging honest kasi you matter to me.

I like you.

Pero, of course, no pressure, no expectations.

With love,
Sid
